FREA PRINTING SHOP

Frea Printing Shop offers a comprehensive range of professional services, catering to all your printing needs. They specialize in high-quality printing, photocopying, and scanning services.




HOW TO GET THERE?

From your location, CvSU gate, head northwest on Mayor's Blvd. Turn left and continue walking until you reach the end of the carmona public market 2. At this point, turn right and you will see the frea printing shop.







AL PRINTING SHOP

Al Printing Shop offers a wide range of reliable services. For those in need of quality laptops or repairs, they also have a selection of second hand laptops available for purchase and provide reliable repair services for existing laptops.




HOW TO GET THERE?

Starting from your current location at CvSU gate, head northwest on Mayor's Blvd. Turn left and continue walking until you reach the end of the carmona public market 2. Then, turn right and you will see the Al printing shop.







JTRI-OH PRINTING

Jtri-Oh Printing is a one-stop shop for all your printing needs. They also offer high-quality t-shirt printing.




HOW TO GET THERE?

From your location, CvSU gate, head northwest on Mayor's Blvd. Turn left and continue walking until you reach the end of the carmona public market 2. At this point, turn right and you're already behind the market. When you reach the end of the market, you will see the jtri-oh printing.







CARMONA MULTI-PURPOSE COOPERATIVE

CMPS provides affordable and high-quality printing services and it is located within the market area. CMPS has a designated table in front of its premises where students can wait while their paper is being printed.




HOW TO GET THERE?

Starting from your current location at CvSU gate, head northwest on Mayor's Blvd. Turn left and continue walking until you reach the carmona public market 2. At this point, go inside the market. When you get there, you will see the CMPS.







SJ SCHOOL AND OFFICE SUPPLIES

This shop offers a wide range of school and office supplies. They offer all high-quality supplies at an affordable price.




HOW TO GET THERE?

From your location, CvSU gate, head northwest on Mayor's Blvd. Turn left and continue walking until you reach the end of the carmona public market 2. Then, turn right and as you continue walking, you will see this school supplies shop.







THE GENERICS PHARMACY

TGP provides a wide range of safe, high-quality, and cost-effective generic medicines, healthcare products, and services. Students can rely on TGP to provide them with the necessary medicines whenever they are unwell.




HOW TO GET THERE?

Starting from your current location at CvSU gate, head northwest on Mayor's Blvd. Turn left and continue walking until you reach the carmona public market 2. Then, you will find TGP on the right side.







CVSU CARMONA REGISTRAR

The registrar can assist students with scheduling or registering for classes in addition to maintaining student records and course data. They also ensure that their academic performance satisfies graduation standards by promptly checking grades with professors.




HOW TO GET THERE?

From your location, CvSU gate, head toward the main building. Behind the main building, you will find the registar.

PARKING LOT

There are two designated parking lots located outside the campus, with one specifically designated for two-wheeled vehicles and another for four-wheeled vehicles. Both parking lots are kept secure, providing students with peace of mind regarding the safety of their vehicles.




HOW TO GET THERE?
