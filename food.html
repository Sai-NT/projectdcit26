DIEREL'S EATERY

Dierel's Eatery provides a wide variety of delicious meals that cater to the varying preferences of the students. Rest assured, your dining experience at Dierel's will be enjoyable and memorable as they strive to provide the best service possible.




HOW TO GET THERE?

From your location, CvSU gate, head northwest on Mayor's Blvd. Turn left and Dierel's location will be on the right.







LOLA AIDA'S EATERY

Lola Aida's Eatery provides a variety of budget-friendly meals. The signature dish offered at this eatery is the delectable bulalo. Additionally, their extensive selection of beverages complements their delectable dishes perfectly, ensuring an exceptional dining experience for all customers.




HOW TO GET THERE?

Starting from your current location at CvSU gate, head northwest on Mayor's Blvd. Turn left and continue walking until you reach the end of the food section. Then, turn right and you will find Lola Aida's Eatery.







JENNISLICIOUS FOOD SERVICE

SILOG AND SIZZLING
