Diriel's Eatery

Dierel's provides a wide variety of delicious meals that cater to the varying preferences of the students. Rest assured, your dining experience at Dierel's will be enjoyable and memorable as they strive to provide the best service possible.

Lola Aida's Eatery

Lola Aida's Eatery provides a variety of budget-friendly meals. The signature dish offered at this eatery is the delectable bulalo.

Jennislicious Food Service

Jennislicious Food Service provides a special menu item - the goto and arrozcaldo with bagnet. They offer five different choices for their customers. This delicious and hearty meal is perfect for students, especially during rainy days. Jennislicious also offers an extensive menu of silog meals and sizzling plates.

Siomai King

You can easily spot Siomai King near the campus - a popular food stall that offers a diverse range of siomai flavors. Siomai King is the ideal choice for those seeking a lighter yet satisfying snack option.

Lola's Hapagkainan

Lola's Hapagkainan not only provides a variety of silog meals but also caters specifically to students by offering affordable options priced at only 50 pesos.

Pazzyon

Pazzyon, a popular food and beverage establishment, offers a diverse selection of delicious snacks. Their store is also aesthetically pleasing, perfect for snapping Insta-worthy photos.

Silog and Sizzling

Silog and Sizzling is a popular dining establishment that prides itself on offering a wide variety of delicious silog and sizzling meals. This special offer includes unlimited rice and free drinks, a perfect option for those looking for a filling yet affordable meal.

Ganda's Eatery

Ganda's Eatery provides an ample selection of delicious and budget-friendly meals. They also maintain a high standard of cleanliness in its surroundings, ensuring that their customers feel comfortable.

Rogel's Fresh Buko Juice

Rogel's Fresh Buko Juice provides a refreshing buko juice and offers a variety of delectable snacks. For those seeking healthier snack options, their store is the perfect choice.

Ice Cream Stores

You can find various ice cream stores near the campus. These ice creams are affordable and will surely satisfy your cravings for sweets.

Bakery

This store offers different kinds of bread and these are very affordable. If you only want a not so heavy food, then this one's for you.

Street Food

You can find numerous street food vendors around the market. If you only have a short break and you want to eat something, then you must visit here, and it will surely satisfy you.

Kakanin Stores

If you're looking for different sticky rice snacks or kakanin, then you must find this store. Their kakanin is very affordable and delicious.
