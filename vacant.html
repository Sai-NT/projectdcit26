STUDENT LOUNGE

These well-appointed spaces offer a comfortable environment for students to utilize during their free time. These lounges are ideal during lunch or snack breaks and for studying during leisure periods.




HOW TO GET THERE?

From the CvSU gate, head towards the security house and turn left. As you walk, you can see the student lounge.







KUBO

It offers students a casual and comfortable space for relaxation and socialization. It provides an opportunity for both academic discussions and stress relief, contributing to a well-rounded and enjoyable school experience.




HOW TO GET THERE?

Starting from the CvSU gate, head towards the security house and turn left. As you walk, you will see the kubo on the left side.







SIDEWALK

Trees near the Hall of Justice creates a pleasant and natural environment, making it an ideal spot for hangouts during vacant hours. The presence of greenery offers a peaceful atmosphere, providing students with a refreshing break from indoor spaces.




HOW TO GET THERE?

From your location, CvSU gate, head northwest on Mayor's Blvd. Turn left and continue walking until you reach the end of the carmona public market. At this point, turn left and you will see this sidewalk.







MINI ARCADE

The mini arcade near the CvSU Carmona offers a vibrant and entertaining space for locals and visitors alike. Nestled beside the bustling market, it provides a lively atmosphere where people can enjoy a variety of arcade games and activities.




HOW TO GET THERE?

Starting from your current location at CvSU gate, head northwest on Mayor's Blvd toward Altarez Rd. Turn left onto Altarez Rd and continue walking until you reach the end of the carmona public market. Then, you will see the mini arcade on the left side.







CHARGING STATION

The area behind the market, near "bato bato," provides a unique and versatile space for various activities. This area offers the perfect blend of accessibility and a more relaxed environment.




HOW TO GET THERE?

From your location, CvSU gate, head northwest on Mayor's Blvd toward Altarez Rd. Turn left onto Altarez Rd and continue walking until you reach the end of the carmona public market. Then, you will see the charging station on the right side.







BATO-BATO

"Bato bato," located near the campus, serves as an ideal hangout spot due to the serene ambiance created by the surrounding trees.




HOW TO GET THERE?

Starting from your current location at CvSU gate, head northwest on Mayor's Blvd toward Altarez Rd. Turn left onto Altarez Rd and continue walking until you reach the end of the carmona public market. Then, turn right and you will see the Bato-Bato.
